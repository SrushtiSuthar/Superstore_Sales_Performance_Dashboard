# Superstore Sales & Operational Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a multi-page Power BI dashboard built using the Superstore Sales dataset. The dashboard analyzes sales trends, category performance, regional profitability, discount impact, and shipping efficiency to generate actionable business insights.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze sales and profit performance
- Identify profitable and loss-making categories
- Evaluate regional business performance
- Analyze discount impact on profitability
- Assess shipping efficiency and operational performance

---

## Dashboard Structure

### Page 1 – Executive Sales Overview
- KPI metrics
- Monthly sales and profit trends

### Page 2 – Category & Regional Performance
- Category-wise sales and profit analysis
- Regional sales and profitability comparison

### Page 3 – Business Insights & Profitability Analysis
- Discount impact on profitability
- Top-performing products
- Sub-category profitability analysis

### Page 4 – Operational & Geographic Analysis
- Shipping performance analysis
- Delivery time comparison
- State-wise sales distribution

---

## Key Business Insights
- Furniture category generated high sales but low profitability
- Moderate discounts improved sales while excessive discounts reduced profit margins
- West region contributed the highest overall profit
- Standard Class shipping had the highest sales volume but longest delivery duration
